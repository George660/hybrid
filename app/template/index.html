Deflationary Yield Generation, Hold & Earn


HIP works by applying a 0.5% fee to each transaction and instantly splitting that fee among all holders of the token.

Holders do not need to stake or wait for fees to be delivered. Fees are awarded by the smart contract and are immediately reflected in the holders balance.



Dashboard Uniswap

Increasing Liquidity

1% from fees creates a pool that automatically adds liquidity to the HIP-ETH pair creating additional liquidity in the process.

Automated buybacks

Every hour 0.5% from fees automatically buy HIP from the market at the current market price. This supports the price level of the token and increases buying pressure. Purchased tokens via this means are forever burnt and removed from the supply, creating a deflationary model in the process

Frictionless yield generation

Hybrid is created to allow frictionless yield generation of assets. DeFi interactions do not always need to be stressful. Hybrid creates a yield generation funnel where even a day newbie can increase their token balance with just holding the asset.
